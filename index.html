I am a Computer Science PhD student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, New York. My interests broadly lie in the areas of Safe and Trustworthy AI and the applications of Natural Language Processing in modelling human emotions. The satisfaction I derive when I am able to solve a problem that has a strong societal impact has always drawn me to this subject. Find more details of my current research here.

Prior to this, I have completed my Master's degree from the University of Calcutta with Dr. Nabendu Chaki as my thesis advisor. I have also received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from St. Xavier's College, Kolkata, India.

Outside my work hours I am an avid reader of biographies, mystery and crime non-fiction. Eating and cooking is what keeps me sane and blissful among all the chaos around!
