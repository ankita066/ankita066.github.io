I am a Computer Science PhD student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, New York advised by Prof. Tomek Strzalkowski. My research interests broadly lie in the areas of Natural Language Processing and Conversational AI. Particularly, I am interested in exploring ways to make digital assistants or applications empathetic towards their users. My research goal is to build emotionally intelligent systems with human-like reasoning capabilities. Find more details of my current research here.

Prior to this, I have completed my Master's degree from the University of Calcutta with Dr. Nabendu Chaki as my thesis advisor. I have also received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from St. Xavier's College, Kolkata, India.

Outside my work hours I am an avid reader of biographies, mystery and crime fiction. Eating and cooking is what keeps me sane and blissful among all the chaos around!
