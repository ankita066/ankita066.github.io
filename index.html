I am a Computer Science PhD student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, New York. My research interests broadly lie in the areas of socio-behavioral feature extraction from text and conversations. I am currently working on the extraction of influence indicators from text and social media content. The satisfaction I derive when I am able to solve a problem that has a strong societal impact has always drawn me to this subject. Find more details of my current research here.

Prior to this, I have completed my Master's degree from the University of Calcutta with Dr. Nabendu Chaki as my thesis advisor. I have also received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from St. Xavier's College, Kolkata, India.

Outside my work hours I am an avid reader of biographies, mystery and crime fiction. Eating and cooking is what keeps me sane and blissful among all the chaos around!
