Research

My research interests broadly lie in the fields of Natural Language Processing and Conversational AI. I am particularly interested in building empathetic dialogue agents for task-oriented systems. Recently, I am exploring the paradigms of step-by-step reasoning and in-context learning to achieve this goal. I have also been working on building tools for efficient analysis of sociolinguistic behaviors from social media.
